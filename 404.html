tinylib.esm demo

A simple vanilla JS router lib on top of window.history. Supports dynamic routes and grouped routes.

  • /tinylib.esm/demo/
  • /tinylib.esm/demo/users/{id}
  • /tinylib.esm/demo/users/{id}/objects/{type}
  • /tinylib.esm/demo/404
  • /admin group routes
  • /tinylib.esm/demo/admin/dashboard
  • /tinylib.esm/demo/admin/panel
  • /tinylib.esm/demo/admin/objects/{id}
